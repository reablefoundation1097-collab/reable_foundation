THE CAMPAIGN

What is Vari?

Vari — the sacred Warkari pilgrimage — draws millions of devotees from across Maharashtra every year to walk hundreds of kilometres to Pandharpur in devotion to Lord Vitthal. It is one of the world's largest religious gatherings and a profound expression of unity, faith, and belonging.

Yet for individuals with physical disabilities, this journey has long been out of reach. Reable Foundation's Vari Campaign exists to change that — arranging wheelchairs, deploying medical teams, and pairing trained volunteers so that no one is left behind.

"Faith knows no disability. Every devotee deserves to walk the path of Vitthal."
